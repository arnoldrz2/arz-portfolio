About Me

Professionally, I come from a background in social work. I have a BA in Psychology as well as several years of experience working for a non-profit, first as a social worker and then as a tech support specialist. Now I spend my time writing code and developing applications.

During my time as a social worker, I worked as a Service Coordinator, helping diverse sets of teams with achieving goals and overcoming obstacles. My strong interpersonal and communication skills allowed me to quickly build rapport with team members and delegate my client’s needs to the team in an effective manner. I also helped my organization improve workflows by assisting with the development of new forms and processes.

Through my work developing forms, I found my way into my organization’s MIS department, where I began working as a System Support Specialist. There my job duties consisted of training staff on how to use relevant applications, creating detailed instructional video guides, implementing new application into the workflow of the organization’s various program teams, and developing the organization's SharePoint websites and structure.

Working on my organization's SharePoint environment inspired me to learn more about web development, which led me to enroll in the Full-Stack Web Development program at UT. Going through the program not only helped me develop the skill set to create and design useful web apps but also sparked a passion for it.
